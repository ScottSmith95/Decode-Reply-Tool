Decode Reply Tool Instructions

Before I go on, I would love to thank you for downloading my plugin. It means quite a lot to me. Feel free to, at any time, contact me on my Twitter or App.net accounts.
This plugin is quite simple to install and operate, but in the event that you held any doubts about how to use it, I have fashioned a little guide of sorts to walk you through its simple setup.

Step One

Sign into your WordPress Dashboard and navigate to the plugins menu. Click the "Add New" button near the page title, as you can see in the image above.

Step Two

Select the "Upload" option in the menu and then select the Decode Reply Tool file that you downloaded (thanks again for that) to your WordPress site. Click "Install Now."

Step Three

When the plugin is finished installing, click "Activate Plugin."

Step Four

You will find the settings for Decode Reply Tool under the "Appearances" section.

Step Five

From here, you can enable or disable the Tool, and add your Twitter and App.net usernames. Be sure to save your changes!

Enjoy
